The Cat Sanctuary is a no-cage, no-kill, lifetime cat sanctuary and adoption center. Our mission is to place rescued cats and kittens into loving, permanent homes; to provide a safe, happy and healthy home for unwanted cats and kittens in a unique, no-cage facility; to prevent pet overpopulation through spaying and neutering; and to educate the public about responsible pet ownership. The Cat Sanctuary is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit corporation, receives no government or public funding, and relies entirely on donations from the public to carry out its mission. Since its founding 10 years ago, The Cat Sanctuary has saved over 8,000 cats (not counting the 13,500 cats we have spayed and neutered!) and currently cares for more than 500 cats and kittens.
